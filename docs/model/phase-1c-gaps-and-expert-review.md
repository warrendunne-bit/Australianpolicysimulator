# Phase 1C unresolved evidence gaps and exploratory relationships

## Unresolved evidence gaps

- Dwelling stock: current model stock retained pending a directly comparable official stock series.
- Household formation: household-size values retained pending ABS household data review.
- Migrant stock: retained pending definition choice such as country of birth, visa stock, citizenship or years since arrival.
- Permanent/temporary/skilled/family/humanitarian migration segmentation: ABS/Home Affairs sources identified but not fully integrated.
- Employment and unemployment by migrant group: not yet modelled.
- Earnings and tax contribution by migrant group: average tax assumption retained pending ATO/Treasury/PBO review.
- Health and education service use by age/status: not yet calibrated.
- Infrastructure cost and lag by geography/asset class: not yet calibrated.
- Rents, vacancy, prices and serviceability: housing stress remains a proxy.
- GDP per person and wages: not directly modelled.
- Emissions/resource use: source identified but numeric pathway not yet integrated.

## Exploratory relationships
